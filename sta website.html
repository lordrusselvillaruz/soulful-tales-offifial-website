About

Soulful Tales is a video game series created by Lord Russel Villaruz. It is a game similar, but also not like other video games. A 2D side-scrolling game with an interesting story, challenging puzzles, and a great soundtrack!



The Story

The story is about an unknown player from another world going on a quest to stop evil from destroying their new home. Making allies along the way and bring back piece once more.

Explore unique and different areas as you progress the world.





Playable Characters



The Unknown Protagonist

A player from another world was sent to save their new home. It is unknown where they originate from or who they are, but they are similar to the land's previous hero. Saving people and stopping evil they will help anyone in need, but side against them and they will be your worst nightmare. They don't give up no matter what happens and they will always find a way to make it happen.





There are more unlockable characters to choose from, all different from one another.
